About

AppOint is a technology company delivering a suite of connected health apps. Their customers include health systems, insurance companies, and (self-insured) employers.

The main service offered by AppOint is the ability to book appointments to a network of licensed physicians for free. They also offer the ability to by appointment with a doctor for a consultation.

AppOint has garnered media coverage in publications including The New York Times, Forbes and TIME. AppOint started its Medical Expert Network with Botswana-licensed Doctors and Pediatrics physicians, which we describes as the largest network of interactive, engaged physicians online.
